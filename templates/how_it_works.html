Step 01

Booking Confirmed

Your request is submitted with the selected service, location, and preferred timing. FixNear then prepares the booking for technician assignment with the right service details in place.

  • Booking details are reviewed clearly
  • Service category and time slot are matched
  • The customer journey starts with cleaner coordination
Step 02

Initial Inspection

The assigned technician arrives with tools, understands the issue on-site, and checks the exact condition before any repair or replacement work begins.

  • The issue is evaluated before work continues
  • Inspection reduces guesswork and pricing confusion
  • Customers get a more transparent service flow
Step 03

Service in Progress

Repair, tightening, cleaning, adjustment, or replacement work is carried out based on the problem found by the technician.

  • Trained technicians handle the technical work
  • The process is more realistic and confidence-building
  • Customers can understand the value of the service
Step 04

Final Testing and Completion

Once the task is finished, the technician checks performance again, confirms the result, and closes the visit with a proper completion flow that feels dependable and customer-ready.

  • Final testing improves trust in the result
  • Customers can see each service step clearly
  • The visit ends with clarity, not uncertainty
